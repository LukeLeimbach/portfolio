Leo Main

Leo Main is a company that sells acoustic wall panneling. My role in this project was to create three widgets.

  • Price calculator - A widget that calculates the price of a wall based on the user's input dimensions.
  • Audio comparison - A widget that compares the audio quality of a room with and without the pannels.
  • Image Slider - A widget that slides to show a before and after of a room with pannels installed.

Wall Music

Wall Music is a music streaming service for Discord. The goal of this project is take what's great about other music bots and streamline the UI/UX to make it more user friendly.

  • The bot iself is made with Discord.js and Node.js with its queue live on Cloud Firestore for realtime queue updates between the bot and web app.
  • The web app is made with React and Ionic hosted on Firebase.
